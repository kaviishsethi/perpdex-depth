Average Depth
Metric
Total order book liquidity (bids + asks) within N basis points from the mid price, measured in base asset units.
Methodology
Sum of all order sizes where price is within ±N BP of mid price. Averaged over the last 30 WebSocket updates (~15 seconds).
Use Case
Higher depth = more liquidity = lower slippage for large orders. Compare exchanges to find the best execution venue for your trade size.

Average Spread
Metric
The difference between the best ask and best bid price, expressed in basis points (0.01% = 1 BP).
Methodology
Spread = (Best Ask - Best Bid) / Mid Price × 10,000. Averaged over the last 30 WebSocket updates.
Use Case
Lower spread = tighter market = lower cost to enter/exit. Critical for high-frequency trading and market making strategies.

Execution Cost
Slippage Calculation
Buy + Sell averaged over 30 updates. We walk through the orderbook simulating fills. Slippage = (AvgFillPrice - MidPrice) / MidPrice × 10,000 bps.
Fee Calculation
Fee = Trade Size × Taker Fee Rate. Rates: Hyperliquid 4.5 bps, EdgeX 3.8 bps, Lighter 0 bps, Paradex 0 bps.
Total Cost
Total = Slippage + Fee in bps. Dollar cost = Trade Size × Total bps / 10,000.
Levels & Depth
Levels = orderbook price levels traversed. Depth Used = actual $ filled from the book.
